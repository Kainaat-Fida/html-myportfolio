The Trending Movies on Netflix

The top 3 movies.

Squid Game

A South Korean survival drama where contestants participate in deadly children's games for a chance to win a massive cash prize, highlighting socio-economic struggles.

The Queen's Gambi

This miniseries follows the journey of an orphaned chess prodigy as she struggles with addiction and rises to the top of the competitive chess world.

Red Notice

A high-octane action-comedy featuring Dwayne Johnson, Gal Gadot, and Ryan Reynolds, where an Interpol agent tracks down the world's most wanted art thief.
